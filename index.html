Frequently Asked Questions

What is sports massage?

Sports massage is a targeted therapy designed to relieve muscle tension, enhance flexibility, and speed up recovery. According to UK sports therapy standards and NHS guidance, it supports pain reduction, improved circulation, and injury prevention for athletes and active individuals.

Is pregnancy massage safe?

Yes, pregnancy massage is safe during pregnancy (after the first trimester) and postpartum, when performed by a qualified therapist. NHS guidelines highlight that it can reduce stress, alleviate back pain, improve circulation, and support recovery after birth.

How do I book a session?

Contact me directly via phone or email to schedule a session at a time and location convenient for you.

How do I prepare for a session?

For sports or deep tissue massage: Hydrate well before and after, wear loose clothing, communicate any injuries or pain areas, and avoid heavy meals beforehand. After the session, rest, stay hydrated, avoid strenuous activities, and apply heat or ice if sore, as recommended by UK physiotherapy guidelines.

 

For pregnancy massage: Consult your GP if high-risk, wear comfortable attire, and inform me of your trimester or postpartum stage. After the session, hydrate, rest, and monitor for any unusual symptoms, following NHS pregnancy care advice.

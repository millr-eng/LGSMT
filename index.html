About me

Hi, I’m Louise, a qualified Sports Massage Therapist based in Colchester. I work with a wide range of clients — from athletes looking to support performance and recovery, to busy parents, desk workers, and anyone needing relief from the physical and mental strain of day-to-day life.

Alongside sports massage, I also offer pregnancy massage and cupping therapy. These treatments allow me to support people at different stages, whether that’s providing safe, soothing relief during pregnancy or using targeted techniques to ease deeper, long-standing tension.

My approach is always personal. I take the time to listen, understand what’s going on for you, and shape each treatment around your needs — with the aim of helping you move more comfortably and feel more at ease in yourself.
